- Link to full post + CTA to follow Instagram

### Paid Promotion Recommendations

**Budget & Timeline:**
- Week 1-2: $50/week testing (5 different post types)
- Week 3+: $150-200/week based on what works
- Total 8-week budget: $800-1,200

**Campaign Setup:**
1. **Awareness Campaign** (40% of budget)
   - Objective: Reach + Impressions
   - Target: Lookalike audiences (email list, website visitors)
   - Age: 22-50 | Interests: trading, finance, startups
   - Ads: Best-performing Reels + educational posts
   - Goal: Get eyeballs + early followers

2. **Traffic Campaign** (35% of budget)
   - Objective: Click Traffic (to website)
   - Target: Engagement audience (people who engaged with your posts)
   - Age: 22-50 | Interests: same as above
   - Ads: Founder story + trial CTA
   - Goal: Drive free trial signups

3. **Conversion Campaign** (25% of budget)
   - Objective: Page Likes + Comments
   - Target: Custom audience (people who visited website but didn't sign up)
   - Ads: Testimonials + "Why I built this" posts
   - Goal: Warm them up to convert to paid tier

**A/B Testing:**
- Test 2-3 versions of each ad (different text, hook, CTA)
- Keep winning versions, pause losers after 3-5 days
- Scale winners 20% per day if ROI is positive

**Success Metrics:**
- Cost per free trial signup: Under $2
- Cost per click: Under $0.30
- Engagement rate on ads: 2%+ (like, comment, share)
